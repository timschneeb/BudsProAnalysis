Galaxy Wearable Privacy Notice

Last updated: December 1, 2020

Samsung Electronics Co., Ltd., along with our affiliates and subsidiaries (collectively, “Samsung”), respect your concerns about privacy. We created this Privacy Notice to make sure you understand how we collect and use your personal information in connection with your Galaxy Wearable. This Privacy Notice does not apply to information collected by other apps, websites, or services provided by Samsung.

The Samsung Privacy Policy for the U.S. at https://www.samsung.com/us/account/privacy-policy (“Samsung Privacy Policy”) also applies to your use of your Galaxy Wearable. Our Samsung Privacy Policy contains more information about how we use and share your data, and the choices available to you regarding our use of the information. It also includes information about how to contact us. Please read our Samsung Privacy Policy in addition to this Privacy Notice. In the event of a conflict between the Samsung Privacy Policy and this Privacy Notice, this Privacy Notice shall prevail. 

WHAT INFORMATION DO WE OBTAIN?

Through your Galaxy Wearable, we may obtain information about you in various ways. 

Information About Your Use of Your Galaxy Wearable

• Device information: Such as your hardware model, device hardware information, serial number and other unique device identifiers, phone number, sales code, current software version, MCC (mobile country code), MNC (mobile network code), MAC address, Bluetooth address, operating system versions, and settings of the devices you use to access your Galaxy Wearable.
• Log information: Diagnostic, technical, error, and usage information such as the time and duration of your use of your Galaxy Wearable, apps you have on your Galaxy Wearable and how you use and interact with the apps and other content offered through your Galaxy Wearable, and any information stored in cookies that we set on your devices.
• Location information: Your device’s GPS signal (with your consent) or information about nearby Wi-Fi access points and cell towers is sent to us (including our service providers), while using your Galaxy Wearable, when you turn on the Location function and use the Find My Watch or Find My Phone function, or use the SOS function.

Your web browser may tell you how to be notified when you receive certain types of cookies or how to restrict or disable certain types of cookies. You can find out how to do this for your particular browser by clicking “help” on your browser’s menu or visiting www.allaboutcookies.org. For mobile devices, you can manage how your device and browser share certain device data by adjusting the privacy and security settings on your device. Your Galaxy Wearable is not designed to respond to “do not track” signals received from browsers. Please note that without cookies you may not be able to use or benefit from all of the features of your Galaxy Wearable.

We also may collect other information about you, your Galaxy Wearable and apps, and your use of your Galaxy Wearable in ways that we describe to you at the time of collection or otherwise with your consent.

HOW DO WE USE YOUR INFORMATION?

We may use the information we collect for the following purposes:

• To help you register for and use your Galaxy Wearable. 
• To provide services and features that you request. 
• To respond to your requests and questions, and to communicate with you about your Galaxy Wearable, including providing assistance when you request technical support related to your Galaxy Wearable. 
• To allow you to interact with select Samsung or third-party apps, features and services. For example, you may be able to back up your apps and settings information to Samsung Cloud or use Samsung Health on your Galaxy Wearable.
• To provide customized content and services related to your Galaxy Wearable. 
• To provide promotions, and offers through direct marketing communications, only where you have given us your separate consent.
• To operate, evaluate and improve our business (including developing new products and services; enhancing and improving our products and services and Galaxy Wearable; managing our communications; analyzing our products and services, consumer base and Galaxy Wearable; aggregating and anonymizing data and performing data analytics; and performing accounting, auditing and other internal functions).
• To ask you for your opinions on our products and services and to carry out customer surveys, with your separate consent if required.
• To provide software updates, maintenance services, and support for your Galaxy Wearable.
• To protect against, identify and prevent fraud and other criminal activity, claims and other liabilities.
• To comply with and enforce applicable legal requirements, relevant industry standards and our policies, including this Galaxy Wearable and Samsung Privacy Policy. 

INFORMATION SHARING
We may share your personal information with (1) our subsidiaries and affiliates and (2) our service providers who perform services on our behalf. We do not authorize our service providers to retain, use or disclose your personal information except as necessary to perform services on our behalf or comply with legal requirements. 

We also may disclose personal information about you (1) if we are required to do so by law or legal process (such as a court order or subpoena); (2) in response to requests by government agencies, such as law enforcement authorities; (3) to establish, exercise or defend our legal rights; (4) when we believe disclosure is necessary or appropriate to prevent physical or other harm or financial loss; (5) in connection with an investigation of suspected or actual illegal activity; or (6) otherwise with your consent. In addition, we may share personal information we collect through your Galaxy Wearable at your request. 

We reserve the right to transfer any personal information we have about you in the event we sell or transfer (or contemplate the sale or transfer of) all or a portion of our business or assets (including in the event of a merger, acquisition, joint venture, reorganization, divestiture, dissolution or liquidation).
